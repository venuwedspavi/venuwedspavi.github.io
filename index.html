About Us

We, referring to Pavitra & Venugopal have decided to live together by sworning the seven vows of wedding in the presense of family, friends & all well wishers. We share loads of common qualities like boldness, futuristic, chilled out, committed, honest, emotional, loving, caring, joyful, socialistic, friendly, selfless & lists goes on & on. Many such things created a strong belief between us & we were able to take forward this bonding to the next level & get married. In the process of knowing & understanding each other we gradually turned out to be a really good friends in fact the best one we could ever get. Cheers to all those mixed moments we had till now & many more to come.
